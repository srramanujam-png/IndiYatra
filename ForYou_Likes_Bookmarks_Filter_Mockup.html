Community Likes & My Saved Items
Consolidates the current 4 stacked sections (Most Liked / Most Saved / My Likes / My Bookmarks) into one unified panel with a tab strip and an entity filter, so any of the likeable/bookmarkable content types can be surfaced without adding more page real estate.
Filter only needs 3 chips — Modules · Lessons · Stories — because liking/bookmarking a Lesson mirrors onto its paired Quiz, and a Story mirrors onto its paired Question. Quizzes and Questions still get their own like/bookmark buttons in the player; they just don't need a separate top-level filter here.
